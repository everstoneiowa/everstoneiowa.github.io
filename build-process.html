We walk you through each phase so you always know where things stand and what comes next.

Common Questions
How long does it take to build a home?
Typically 4–6 months from breaking ground to closing, depending on the plan, finish selections, and inspection scheduling. We'll give you a realistic timeline upfront.
Can I make changes once construction starts?
Some changes are possible early in the build, but others aren't once certain phases are complete. We'll be upfront about what's feasible and what it costs before anything changes.
Do you work with outside lenders?
Yes. You're free to use any lender you choose. We can also connect you with local lenders who have experience with new construction financing if that's helpful.
What's covered under warranty?
We provide a standard builder's warranty covering workmanship and materials. Iowa also has statutory protections for new construction. We'll walk you through all of it at closing.
What if something comes up after I move in?
Call or text us directly. We stand behind our work and will address any legitimate warranty issues promptly. You're not dealing with a warranty department. You're calling the builder.
